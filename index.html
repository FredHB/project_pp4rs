Webscraping and analyzing global eBay price data for selected used goods.

We webscrape price data from past auctions of used, undifferentiated goods sold in different Western countries by non-professional eBay sellers. Our focal good is the Apple iPhone X with 64GB of storage. We find evidence of heterogeneous sales prices, and diverging bidding behavior—measured by the average number of bids per auction—by country. We do not detect outcome differences between ascending eBay auctions and fixed price listings.
